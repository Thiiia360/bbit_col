About Us

Kibiko Primary School is a public mixed-day primary school located near Ngong in Kajiado County, Kenya. The African Inland Church (AIC) supports it by helping with management and spiritual guidance. The school follows Kenya’s Competency-Based Education (CBE) system, which emphasizes the development of practical skills, talents, and values. It offers both academic and co-curricular activities that aim to nurture responsible, well-rounded, and skilled learners.
